Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry

I remember it was a bright Sunday afternoon in Brighton, London after it had rained about an hour ago. I had just left the Leaky Cauldron, a cozy cafe that I visit quite often when I need to focus on my school assignments.
The time on my wrist watch showed 3:18 PM as I remembered that I needed to be home at 3:30 to babysit my nephew and niece, who were only 2 years old.
I started to walk a tad bit faster and arrived at my porch within minutes. As I approached my front door, I saw a tan envelope with my name written in black ink and with a bright red H stamp seal.
I wondered why this letter would be sent to me and this is what it read…

Dear Computer Scientist,
We are pleased to inform you that you have a place at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
Below find which house you would like to be sorted in.

Yours sincerely,
Minerva McGonagall
